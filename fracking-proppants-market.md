<p><p>Fracking proppants are materials used to keep fractures open during hydraulic fracturing, enhancing oil and gas extraction. The market is categorized into three types: low-density, medium-density, and high-density proppants. Low-density proppants, often made from materials like resin-coated sands, provide effective support with reduced weight. Medium-density proppants offer a balance between strength and weight, while high-density proppants, typically composed of ceramic or sintered materials, provide superior strength and conductivity, making them suitable for high-pressure environments. Each type caters to specific well conditions and performance requirements.</p></p>
