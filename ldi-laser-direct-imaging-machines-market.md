<p><strong>The LDI (Laser Direct Imaging) Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygon Mirror 365nm</li><li>DMD 405nm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDI (Laser Direct Imaging) machines market is categorized into two main types based on technology: Polygon Mirror at 365nm and DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) at 405nm. The Polygon Mirror system utilizes high-speed rotation to scan laser beams for precise imaging; it’s ideal for applications requiring fine detail at shorter wavelengths. Conversely, DMD technology leverages micro-mirrors to modulate light for pattern generation, offering high resolution and flexibility, making it suitable for diverse industries like electronics and printed circuit boards.</p></p>
