<p><p>The Airborne Hyperspectral Imaging System market comprises various types based on spectral range. Visible/Near-Infrared (VNIR) systems capture light from 400 to 1000 nm, ideal for vegetation and mineral analysis. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) ranges from 1000 to 2500 nm, useful in detecting moisture and organic materials. Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) covers 3000 to 5000 nm, applying to thermal imaging. Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) spans 8000 to 14000 nm, effective for thermal signatures. Other technologies include UV-Vis and Fourier Transform Infrared (FT-IR), offering diverse applications.</p></p>
