<p><ul><li>Dynamic Wireless Charging Systems</li><li>Stationary Wireless Charging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless charging systems for electric vehicles (EVs) are categorized into two main types: dynamic and stationary. Dynamic wireless charging systems allow vehicles to charge while in motion, utilizing embedded infrastructure to transfer energy through electromagnetic fields, enhancing convenience and range. In contrast, stationary wireless charging systems require the vehicle to be parked over a charging pad, where energy is transferred to the battery. Both types aim to improve the EV charging experience and support the broader adoption of electric mobility.</p></p>
