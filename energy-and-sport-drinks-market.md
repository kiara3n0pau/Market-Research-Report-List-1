<p><p>The energy and sports drinks market is divided into three main types based on their carbohydrate concentrations. Hypotonic sports drinks contain low carbohydrate levels, offering quick hydration and energy with minimal calories, ideal for rapid fluid replacement. Isotonic sports drinks balance hydration and energy, providing hydration and replenishing electrolytes and carbohydrates, making them suitable for moderate to intense activities. Hypertonic sports drinks have high carbohydrate content, designed for rapid energy replenishment, often used after prolonged exercise sessions. Each type caters to specific hydration and energy needs during physical activities.</p></p>
