<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Exhaust Emission Systems market encompasses technologies designed to reduce harmful emissions from heavy-duty vehicles. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) systems utilize a urea-based solution to convert nitrogen oxides (NOx) into harmless nitrogen and water vapor, enhancing efficiency and compliance with regulations. Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) systems recirculate a portion of the engine's exhaust back into the combustion chamber, lowering NOx emissions by reducing combustion temperatures. Both systems are vital for meeting stringent emissions standards and improving environmental sustainability.</p></p>
