<p><p>The crosslinked polyethylene (PEX) tube market is segmented into three main types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa is produced through an extra-peroxide process, offering superior flexibility and high thermal resistance. PE-Xb is made via a silane method, providing good mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals. PE-Xc, created using radiation methods, is known for its excellent strength and durability. Each type serves distinct applications in plumbing, heating, and insulation, catering to various industry needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
