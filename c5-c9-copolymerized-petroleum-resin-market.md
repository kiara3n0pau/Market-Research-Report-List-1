<p><p>The C5 and C9 copolymerized petroleum resin market is divided into two primary types: cold polymer resin and thermo polymer resin. Cold polymer resins are typically used in applications requiring flexibility and low-temperature performance, making them suitable for adhesives and sealants. In contrast, thermo polymer resins are heat-sensitive and provide excellent thermal stability, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and packaging, enhancing product performance and durability.</p></p>
