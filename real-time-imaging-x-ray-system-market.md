<p><p>The Real-Time Imaging X-Ray System market is primarily categorized into two types: Flat Panel Detector (FPD) real-time imaging detection technology and Linear Array Detector real-time imaging detection technology. Flat panel detectors utilize a flat surface to convert X-rays into digital images, offering high-resolution outputs and improved image quality with reduced radiation exposure. In contrast, Linear Array Detectors employ a linear configuration of sensor elements to capture X-ray data quickly, enabling rapid image acquisition for real-time diagnostics and treatment assessment.</p></p>
